## Navigating the Official Study in Saudi Portal Gateway

The modern admission framework relies entirely on a single online destination: the [Study in Saudi gateway](https://studyinsaudi.moe.gov.sa/). This unified portal tracks and processes your files through automated filters before your profile ever reaches a human admissions board.

### The 4-Stage Application Processing Sequence

To ensure your application transitions smoothly through the backend review system, you must follow this exact sequence:

1. **System Registration & Authentication:** Create your profile using a primary email address. You must click the confirmation link within a 24-hour window, or the system locks out your passport data to prevent duplicate registrations.
2. **Biometric Identification:** Enter your core biographical information. Every character of your legal name, place of birth, and nationality must match your passport exactly. Minor typing errors can lead to immediate complications during the subsequent visa issuance stage.
3. **Document Verification & Language Integration:** Upload your academic achievements and civil paperwork. This is where you insert your original credentials alongside their required translations.
4. **Target Selection:** Rank your top university preferences. The portal allows you to select up to 5 universities and define your intended fields of study.

### Essential Academic and Civil Paperwork Checklist

* **High School Degree & Transcripts:** For undergraduate tracks, your verified high school diploma or intermediate certificate is the foundational piece of your portfolio. It must be attested by your local educational boards.
* **Police Character Certificate:** An official document issued by your local law enforcement demonstrating a clean background record. This document must be issued within six months of your submission window.
* **Health Certificate / Medical Fitness Report:** A certified medical assessment confirming you are free from communicable or contagious diseases, bearing the signature and stamp of a recognized medical practitioner.
