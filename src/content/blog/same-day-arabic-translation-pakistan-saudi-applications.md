*   Missing an application deadline

For this reason, same day translation is not just about speed. It is about getting the documents right the first time without losing valuable time.

## What Counts as an Urgent Translation Request?

An urgent request is any document translation that needs to be completed within a very short timeframe, usually on the same day or within 24 hours.

Common urgent cases include:

### Saudi visa deadlines
If your visa submission is due soon, you may need immediate translation of educational or personal documents.

### University admission deadlines
Many students apply to Saudi universities at the last minute and need transcripts, degrees, and certificates translated fast.

### Scholarship submission deadlines
Scholarship applications often require multiple documents, and missing one translation can block the whole application.

### Last-minute embassy document requests
Sometimes the embassy asks for additional translated paperwork after the application has already started.

## Which Documents Can Usually Be Translated Same Day?

Not every document takes the same amount of time, but many common papers can often be translated quickly if the original scan is clear and complete.

### Degrees and transcripts
These are among the most common documents for Saudi students. They can often be handled on the same day if the format is straightforward.

### Birth certificates and ID documents
Short personal documents are usually faster to process because they contain less text.

### Passports and visa papers
These documents are often urgent and can usually be translated quickly when the image quality is good.

### Affidavits and supporting letters
These may take slightly longer if they contain complex legal wording, but they are still often suitable for fast turnaround.

> [!TIP]
> A simple rule applies here: the clearer the document, the faster the translation.

## Tips to Avoid Delays Before You Send Documents

A lot of delay happens before translation even starts. You can save time by preparing properly.

Follow these tips:

*   Send clear, high-resolution scans or photos
*   Make sure all corners of the document are visible
*   Check spelling of your name across all documents
*   Group documents in one message instead of sending them one by one
*   Tell the translator your deadline upfront

The fewer corrections needed, the faster the final delivery.
