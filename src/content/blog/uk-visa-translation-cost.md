Wondering how much a certified translation costs for a UK visa application? This guide explains UKVI translation pricing, cost factors, document types, and how to avoid paying more than necessary.

Applying for a UK visa often involves more than completing forms and gathering supporting documents. If your documents are not in English or Welsh, you'll usually need a certified translation that meets UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) requirements.

One of the most common questions applicants ask is: **How much does UK visa translation cost?**

The answer depends on several factors, including document type, language pair, turnaround time, and certification requirements. In this guide, we'll break down average pricing, explain what affects translation costs, and help you choose a reliable provider without overspending.

## What Factors Affect UK Visa Translation Pricing?

Not all documents cost the same to translate. Several variables influence the final price.

### Language Pair
Some language pairs are more common and therefore less expensive.
Examples:
* Urdu to English
* Arabic to English
* Spanish to English

Rare languages may require specialist translators and higher fees.

### Document Length
Many translation providers charge:
* Per word
* Per page
* Per document

A one-page birth certificate costs far less than a 15-page academic transcript.

### Urgent Delivery
Need your translation within 24 hours? Rush services typically increase pricing due to priority handling.

### Certification Requirements
UKVI-compliant certified translations require:
* Certification statement
* Translator information
* Accuracy declaration

These additional requirements can affect pricing at some agencies, though Lisan.pk includes them as standard.

## UK Visa Translation Cost by Document Type

### Birth Certificate Translation Cost
Birth certificates are among the most frequently translated documents for:
* Student visas
* Dependent visas
* Spouse visas
* Settlement applications

Because they are generally short, costs remain relatively affordable, usually around **€15**.

### Marriage Certificate Translation Cost
Marriage certificates are commonly required for spouse and family visas.
Pricing typically depends on:
* Number of pages
* Language complexity
* Certification requirements

### Academic Transcript Translation Cost
Academic records often cost more because they contain:
* Multiple subjects
* Grades
* Credits
* Institutional terminology

These documents require careful formatting and verification, pushing costs closer to **€20–€25**.

### Bank Statement Translation Cost
Financial evidence can involve multiple pages, making costs higher than simple certificates.

## Can You Use Google Translate or AI Translation for UK Visa Documents?

Many applicants wonder whether free tools can replace professional translation services.

While machine translation has improved significantly, UKVI expects translated documents to be accurate and independently verifiable.

Common problems with AI-generated translations include:
* Incorrect legal terminology
* Formatting errors
* Missing context
* Mistranslated names or places

For official immigration submissions, professional review remains the safest option.

## Certified Translation vs Notarized Translation

Applicants frequently confuse certified and notarized translations.

In most cases, UKVI focuses on certification requirements rather than notarization.

A certified translation generally includes:
* Translator declaration
* Certification statement
* Contact details

Notarization serves a different purpose and is often unnecessary.
