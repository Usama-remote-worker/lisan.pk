## Complete List of Required Documents for Saudi Scholarship (Bachelor & PhD)

Below is the official Saudi scholarship document checklist for 2026. Tick off each item.



### 1. Educational Certificates & Transcripts
- **Bachelor applicants**: Matric + FSc/intermediate certificates + mark sheets
- **PhD applicants**: Bachelor’s + Master’s degrees and transcripts
- Must be attested by [HEC (Higher Education Commission)](https://www.hec.gov.pk/english/services/students/Attestation/Pages/default.aspx) and IBCC.

### 2. Passport & National ID Copies
- Valid passport (minimum 18 months validity from scholarship start date)
- Copy of CNIC (Computerized National Identity Card) or B-Form for younger applicants
- 4 recent passport-size photos (white background)

### 3. Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 500–800 words explaining why you want to study in Saudi Arabia
- Must match your chosen field (engineering, medicine, Islamic studies, etc.)

### 4. Recommendation Letters
- 2–3 letters from professors or employers
- On official letterhead, signed and stamped

### 5. Language Proficiency Proof (IELTS/TOEFL)
- **IELTS Academic**: minimum 5.5–6.0 for bachelor; 6.5+ for PhD. See [IELTS Academic requirements](https://www.britishcouncil.pk/).
- **TOEFL iBT**: 70+ (bachelor) / 90+ (PhD)
- Some Saudi universities may require Arabic proficiency – check your program.

### 6. Research Proposal (for PhD applicants)
- 1,500–2,000 words including methodology, literature review, and objectives
- Must be aligned with Saudi Vision 2030 research priorities

### 7. Medical Report & Police Clearance Certificate
- Medical checkup from a recognized hospital (hepatitis, HIV, chest X-ray)
- Police character certificate from your local district police office

> [!WARNING]
> **Critical note**: All documents originally in English or Urdu must be translated into Arabic by a certified translation service. Saudi embassies and universities reject non-Arabic documents.
