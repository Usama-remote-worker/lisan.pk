## 1. King Abdullah University of Science & Technology (KAUST)

KAUST is the highest-ranked Saudi institution in global league tables and the most financially generous—but also the most competitive.

### Scholarship Type & Financial Benefits
Every admitted graduate student automatically receives the **KAUST Fellowship**, a fully funded package with no separate scholarship application. The verified 2026 benefits include:

- **Full tuition support** (valued at approximately USD 35,000/year)
- **Annual living allowance**: USD 20,000–30,000 depending on degree level
- **Free on-campus housing** with utilities and WiFi
- **Comprehensive medical and dental coverage**
- **Relocation support** including travel expenses

### Eligibility & Academic Requirements (2026)
- **Degree level**: Master’s (MS), MS/PhD track, and PhD only
- **English proficiency**: TOEFL iBT minimum 81 or IELTS minimum 6.5
- **Application deadlines**: Round 3 deadline is **11 April 2026**.

**Required Documents & Translation Need:** KAUST requires official academic transcripts and graduation certificates. For Pakistani students whose degrees or transcripts are in Urdu, KAUST mandates **certified English translations**.

## 2. King Abdulaziz University (KAU), Jeddah

KAU is one of the most popular destinations for Pakistani scholarship applicants because it offers programs at all levels—bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD—with rolling admissions.

### Scholarship Coverage & Stipend Breakdown

| Benefit | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tuition fees** | Fully covered |
| **Monthly stipend (UG)** | SAR 850 (~USD 225) |
| **Monthly stipend (Master's)** | SAR 900 (~USD 240) |
| **Monthly stipend (PhD)** | SAR 1,900 (~USD 505) |
| **Accommodation** | Free on-campus housing |
| **Health insurance** | Covered for student and family |
| **Airfare** | Round-trip travel support |

### Eligibility & Language Requirements
- **Age limits**: Under 25 (bachelor’s), under 30 (master’s), under 35 (PhD).
- **Application window**: KAU accepts applications throughout the year on a rolling basis.

**Required Documents & Translation Need:** All documents not originally in Arabic or English must be accompanied by a **certified translation**. For most Pakistani students, this means Urdu-to-Arabic for undergraduate and Urdu-to-English for graduate programs.

## 3. King Saud University (KSU), Riyadh

Founded in 1957, KSU is Saudi Arabia’s oldest university and a central pillar of the Kingdom’s Vision 2030 educational strategy.

### Scholarship Benefits & Vision 2030 Alignment
KSU’s 2026 scholarship initiative targets high-achieving graduate students globally. PhD programs include:
- Full tuition fee waiver
- Monthly living stipend
- Full medical care at university hospitals
- Graduates may work in Saudi Arabia for one year after completion

### Eligibility & Deadlines
- **Undergraduate**: SAT score of at least 1010; age 17–25.
- **PhD**: TOEFL minimum 61 (iBT) or IELTS minimum 6.0.
- **PhD application deadline**: **15 May 2026**.

**Required Documents & Translation Need:** KSU explicitly states that all certificates must be properly attested. **Translation into Arabic or English from a certified translator** is a prerequisite for attestation.

## 4. Islamic University of Madinah (IUM)

IUM remains the top choice for Pakistani students pursuing Islamic studies alongside modern disciplines.

### Scholarship Coverage for Pakistani Students
- Full tuition fee coverage
- Free furnished housing
- Regular monthly stipend
- Arrival allowance and academic bonuses

### Eligibility & 2026 Application Deadlines
- **Nationality**: International Muslim students, including Pakistanis.
- **Age**: 17–25 years for undergraduate programs.
- **Application deadlines**: Bachelor’s: **14 June 2026**; Postgrad: **31 October 2026**.

**Required Documents & Translation Need:** Critical rule: “All documents must be in Arabic or English”. Since most Pakistani academic documents are in Urdu, **mandatory certified Arabic translation** is the first step.

## 5. Umm Al-Qura University (UQU), Makkah

UQU is ranked #622 in QS World University Rankings 2026 and enrolls over 45,000 students.

### Scholarship Options
- **External scholarships**: For non-Saudi students applying from outside Saudi Arabia.
- Available at bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD levels.

### Eligibility & Institutional Background
- **Age**: 17–25 years for undergraduate applicants.
- International students must provide proof of Arabic or English proficiency.

**Required Documents & Translation Need:** Applicants need transcripts, passport copy, and recommendation letters. All documents originally in Urdu require **certified Arabic or English translation**.

## The Document Translation Rule That Applies to All Five Universities

Across every institution ranked above, one requirement is identical: any document not originally in Arabic or English must be accompanied by a **certified translation** bearing the translator’s official seal and signature.

### Common Rejection Triggers:
- Submitting self-translated documents without a certified stamp.
- Using a translator not recognized by the Saudi Cultural Attaché.
- Missing the attestation step that follows translation.
