## Fully Funded Scholarships With Active Deadlines Right Now

Two major Saudi scholarship programs share the same upcoming deadline: **May 21, 2026**. Both are fully funded, cover all major expenses, and are open to international students.

### 1. Saudi Government Scholarship (Study in Saudi) – Deadline: May 21, 2026
This is the flagship initiative of the Ministry of Education, offering places at over 27 public universities across the Kingdom.

*   **Coverage:** Full tuition, monthly living stipend, free university housing, comprehensive healthcare, and an annual round-trip flight ticket.
*   **Eligibility:** Non-Saudi nationals. Age limits: 17–25 for bachelor’s, under 30 for master’s, under 35 for PhD.
*   **Where to apply:** [Official Study in Saudi Portal](https://studyinsaudi.moe.gov.sa/)

**Why apply now?** You have 20 days to prepare and submit your documents. Ensure your [educational certificates are translated and certified](/services/translation) before uploading.

### 2. Islamic University of Madinah Scholarship – Deadline: May 21, 2026
A deeply respected scholarship for international Muslim students, located in the holy city of Madinah.

*   **Coverage:** Full tuition waiver, monthly stipend, free furnished accommodation, quality healthcare, and free yearly transportation to the Prophet’s Mosque.
*   **Eligibility:** Male Muslim students only. Undergraduate applicants must be 17–25 years old.
*   **Where to apply:** [IUM Admissions Portal](https://admission.iu.edu.sa/)

> **Key Reminder:** Both programs require you to submit all documents, including [certified Arabic translations](/services/translation), by the May 21 deadline. No late applications are accepted.
