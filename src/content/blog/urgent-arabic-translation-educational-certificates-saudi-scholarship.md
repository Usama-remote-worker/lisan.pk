## What “Certified Arabic Translation” Actually Means for Saudi Universities in 2026

Not all translations are equal. A screenshot from Google Translate or a text file from an unregistered translator will get your application cancelled. Saudi universities, their scholarship committees, and the Saudi Cultural Attaché require:

1.  A translator’s official stamp and signature on every page
2.  A signed certificate of accuracy stating the translation is true and faithful
3.  **Matching formatting** — the Arabic translation mirrors the original document layout
4.  Professional registration details of the translator or agency
