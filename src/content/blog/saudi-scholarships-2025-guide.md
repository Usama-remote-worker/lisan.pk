## 📂 The "Documentation Trap" (What You MUST Have)

You can't just upload a photo of your degree. For a Saudi Scholarship, your papers must be "Embassy Ready."

### 1. The Mandatory Documents:
- **Degree & Transcripts:** Must be HEC attested.
- **Police Character Certificate:** To prove your clean record.
- **Medical Fitness Report:** From a recognized hospital.
- **Arabic Translation:** **THIS IS CRITICAL.** English documents are often rejected. You must have a [Certified Arabic Translation](/services/translation/academic-degree-transcript).

### 2. The Chain of Attestation:
Don't get confused by the process. Follow this sequence:
1. **HEC Attestation** (For Education docs)
2. **MOFA Attestation** (The Ministry stamp)
3. **Arabic Translation** (By a [Certified Expert like Lisan.pk](/services/translation))
4. **Embassy Stamping** (The final hurdle)
