## Introduction

Start with a strong hook using the **Hisham Sarwar** methodology:
1. **Identify the pain point**: What is the reader struggling with?
2. **Provide immediate value**: Give a quick answer or overview.
3. **Promise a solution**: Tell them what they will learn by reading the rest of the post.

## The Core Problem

Explain the "why" and "how". Break the content down into readable chunks. Use bullet points:
- Key point 1
- Key point 2
- Key point 3
