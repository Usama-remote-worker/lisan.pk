## What Defines "Certified" Translation in Pakistan?

A certified translation is a document accompanied by a formal statement from a professional translator or agency. This statement—often called a "Certificate of Accuracy"—attests that the translation is a true and faithful representation of the original. For the document to be valid in official circles, it must be signed and stamped by a recognized agency. This is the hallmark of the certified translation services Pakistan provided by Lisan.pk.
